About Us

Welcome To Wings & Wonders Child Development Centre

Helping Children Grow, Learn, And Thrive

At Wings & Wonders Child Development Centre, we are dedicated to supporting children in reaching their fullest potential through compassionate care, expert guidance, and individualized developmental programs. We believe that every child is unique and deserves the opportunity to grow with confidence, independence, and joy.

Our team works closely with children and their families to address developmental, communication, learning, behavioral, and social challenges in a nurturing and supportive environment. Through evidence-based approaches and personalized intervention plans, we help children build essential life skills and achieve meaningful progress.

We are committed to creating a safe and encouraging space where children can explore their abilities, celebrate their strengths, and overcome challenges. Together with families, we strive to build brighter futures and unlock endless possibilities for every child who walks through our doors.

Our Approach

Evidence-Based Care For Every Child

We use individualized, evidence-based intervention plans tailored to each child's unique strengths, needs, and goals.

Our multidisciplinary team collaborates across speech, occupational, behavioral, and physiotherapy to provide holistic support.

We actively involve parents and families in the therapy process, ensuring progress continues at home and in the community.

Speech Therapy

Helping children develop clear communication, language skills, and confidence in expressing themselves.
Occupational Therapy

Supporting children to build everyday skills, fine motor abilities, and sensory processing capabilities.
Physiotherapy

Improving children's movement, strength, and physical development through targeted therapy exercises.
Behavioural Therapy

Guiding children to understand emotions, manage behaviour, and develop positive social interactions.
Special Education

Individualized learning support that helps children with diverse needs achieve their academic and personal goals.
Sensory Integration

Helping children process and respond to sensory information effectively for better daily functioning.

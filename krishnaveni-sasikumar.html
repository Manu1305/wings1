Krishnaveni Sasikumar P

RCI Licensed Special Educator | Educational Therapist | Counsellor
Qualification B.Ed. Special Education (IDD), PG Diploma in Psychology & Counselling
Areas of Expertise Special Education Educational Therapy Child Counselling
Registration RCI Licensed Professional
Experience 11+ Years | 1,000+ Children Supported
Language(s) English / Malayalam

Krishnaveni Sasikumar P is an experienced Special Educator with over 11 years of professional experience in the field of special education and child development. She holds a B.Ed. in Special Education (Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities) from KVM College of Special Education, affiliated with University of Kerala (2014–2015 batch), and is a licensed professional registered with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI).

She has also completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Psychology and Counselling, enhancing her expertise in understanding children's developmental, emotional, and behavioral needs. Throughout her career, she has provided educational and therapeutic support to more than 1,000 children with diverse developmental and learning needs, helping them achieve meaningful academic, social, and functional outcomes.

Professional Development & Workshops

Krishnaveni has actively participated in several professional development programs and workshops, including:

International Workshop N'ABLE

Training Workshop on Comprehensive Childhood Disability Management

Workshop on Learning Disabilities conducted by the Institute of Child Development Studies and Composite Regional Centre Kozhikode

Approach & Practice

She regularly conducts group therapy programs, attention-training sessions, and parent counselling workshops, adopting evidence-based and child-centered approaches to support the holistic development of children with special needs. Her dedication, compassion, and extensive experience make her a trusted professional in the field of special education and rehabilitation.

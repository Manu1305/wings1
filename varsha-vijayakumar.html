Varsha Vijayakumar

Occupational Therapist | Pediatric & Rehabilitation Specialist
Qualification Bachelor of Occupational Therapy (B.O.T.)
Areas of Expertise Pediatric OT Sensory Processing ASD & ADHD Neuro Rehabilitation
Experience In Pediatric, Neurological, Orthopedic, Mental Health, Oncology
Focus Child Development & Functional Independence
Language(s) English / Malayalam

Varsha Vijayakumar is a qualified Occupational Therapist with a Bachelor's degree in Occupational Therapy and clinical experience across pediatric, neurological, orthopedic, mental health, oncology, and rehabilitation settings. She has worked with children presenting with developmental delays, Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), ADHD, sensory processing difficulties, learning disabilities, and other developmental challenges.

With experience in multidisciplinary child development clinics, she focuses on improving children's functional abilities, independence, learning, play skills, sensory processing, attention, and participation in daily activities. Varsha is committed to providing evidence-based, child-centered interventions while working closely with families to support each child's developmental journey.

Areas of Clinical Focus

Developmental Delays — fine motor, gross motor, and self-care skill development

Sensory Processing Difficulties — sensory integration therapy for over- or under-responsive children

ASD & ADHD — attention, behavioural regulation, social participation, and daily routine support

Learning Disabilities — handwriting, visual-motor integration, and school readiness skills

Approach & Practice

"Helping children reach their fullest potential through meaningful and engaging therapeutic interventions."

Varsha adopts a family-centred, occupation-based approach, ensuring that therapy goals are meaningful and directly relevant to each child's daily life. She collaborates closely with parents, teachers, and the multidisciplinary team to create holistic intervention plans that promote lasting outcomes and empower children to thrive.
